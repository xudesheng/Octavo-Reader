Octavo Reader Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2025

Privacy First Promise: Octavo Reader is designed with your privacy as the top priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage analytics.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app, which documents you open, or your reading patterns
  • Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, operating system details, or hardware specifications
  • Location Data: We do not access or collect any location information
  • User Accounts: We do not maintain user accounts or authentication systems

2. How Your Documents Are Processed

Local Processing

  • PDF documents are processed entirely on your device using Apple's PDFKit framework
  • Text extraction and OCR (when needed) are performed locally using Apple's Vision framework
  • Document content never leaves your device unless explicitly sent for AI translation

AI Translation and Summary Services

  • When you use the AI translation feature, only the selected text paragraph is sent to your chosen AI service provider
  • When you use the AI summary feature, only the extracted text content (limited to 100,000 characters by default, configurable up to 200,000 in settings) is sent to generate a summary
  • We never send the complete PDF document, images, or any document metadata
  • All text extraction is performed locally on your device using Apple's PDFKit
  • You must provide your own API keys for AI services (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.)
  • Your API keys are stored securely in your device's Keychain and never transmitted to us

3. Third-Party AI Services

When you choose to use AI translation or summary features:

  • Selected text is sent directly from your device to your chosen AI provider using your API key
  • Octavo Reader acts only as a conduit - we do not process, store, or access this data
  • Each AI provider has their own privacy policy governing how they handle the text you send
  • You can disable AI features entirely and use Octavo Reader as a local-only PDF reader

4. Data Storage

  • Local Only: All app data is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not sync or backup any data to our servers
  • Translation Cache: Translation results may be cached locally to improve performance - this data never leaves your device
  • Settings: Your preferences and API key configurations are stored locally in your device's secure storage

5. Network Usage

Octavo Reader connects to the internet only for:

  • Downloading PDF documents from arXiv or other URLs you provide
  • Sending selected text to AI services when you explicitly request translation or summary generation (using your API keys)
  • Checking for app updates through the App Store

6. Permissions

Octavo Reader may request the following permissions:

  • File Access: To open PDF documents from your device, iCloud, or other file providers
  • Network Access: To download PDFs from URLs and communicate with AI services
  • No other permissions are required or requested

7. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, our app is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Data Security

  • All local data is protected by iOS security features including device encryption
  • API keys are stored in iOS Keychain with appropriate security attributes
  • No data is transmitted to Octavo Reader servers because we don't operate any servers

9. Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all associated data
  • You can disable AI features and use Octavo Reader as a completely offline PDF reader
  • You control which AI services to use through your own API key configuration

10. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date and notify users through app updates. Given our no-data-collection approach, any changes will maintain the same privacy-first principles.

11. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, you can contact us through:

  • App Store review system
  • GitHub repository issues (if you're a technical user)
Summary: Octavo Reader is designed to be a completely private PDF reader. We don't collect any data about you or your usage. When you use AI features, only the text you select is sent to AI services using your own API keys. Everything else stays on your device.
