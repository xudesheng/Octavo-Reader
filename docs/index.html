üìã Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open a PDF from arXiv?
You can either: 1) Use the built-in arXiv browser by tapping the arXiv button, 2) Enter an arXiv ID or URL in the document picker, or 3) Use the share extension from Safari.
How does AI translation work?
Simply tap on any paragraph in the PDF. Octavo will extract the text locally and send only the paragraph text (not the entire PDF) to the LLM for translation. The translation appears as an overlay.
Is my data private?
Yes! Octavo only sends necessary text snippets to AI services for translation/summarization. The original PDF files never leave your device.
Which AI models are supported?
Octavo supports OpenAI (GPT-4, GPT-4o, o1), Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, and Azure OpenAI. You can configure your preferred service in Settings.
Can I use Octavo without an internet connection?
Yes for basic PDF reading. AI features (translation and summarization) require an internet connection and API keys.
